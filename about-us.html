Hagan Lubricants

Who are we?

Hagan Lubricants is a leading organization based in UAE and was founded in 1910 by our honored founder late Haji Momin khan as a trading company dealing with dry fruits, spices, tea and textile. Hagan Lubricants Industry was established in 1995 with a state of the artificiality for lubricants and edible oil Jafza in Dubai. Over the years the operations of the Hagan Lubricants have expanded having offices across south east Asia and middle east. Currently our products are being exported to all over Middle East, South-east Asia, Africa and CIS countries.

In the last few years Hagan Lubricants has diversified its business and has grown in the lubricant industry and started its operations worldwide by the name of HAGAN Lubricants.

Mission Statement

Hagan Lubricants believes on three principles which are mandatory for us to grow people, quality and commitment.

People

We believe that our employees are more important to us than our customers. Why? Because we think that if employees are happy and motivated then they will be able to give their best and deliver the best output required to achieve the best quality in our products, which will eventually help us to deliver the best to our respected consumers and in return gain their trust, satisfaction and happiness. So for that we maintain a very friendly and a supportive corporate environment for our employees and we believe in employee development because a happy employee means a happy customer. If we summarize, it is obvious that employee development is very important that helps us to achieve and grow our customer base by providing them the best quality products which brings us to our second principle.

Quality

Our basic principle for quality of our products and services that's meets the needs and expectations of our customers; Hagan Lubricants quality management system, takes care of health, safety, and environmental standards.

Commitment

We strongly believe in commitment, We can achieve our vision only by being focused and committed to our future goals. Our staff works with full commitment delivering good quality products and achieving customer satisfaction.
