HAGAN - 4T - J

10W40 - SJ (JASO MA)

Hagan SJ (JASO MA) 10W-40 is modern performance four-stroke multi-graded motorcycle engine oil. It is used for both liquid and air cooled motorcycle engines that are performing under extensive conditions and extreme operations. It is suitable for motorcycles with or without immersed clutches and combined engines/transmission units or even separate gear boxes where multi-grade engine oil can be used.

Moreover, 10W40 JASO MA is applicable in motorcycles having exhaust catalytic converters and back torque limiters. This oil is also used for four-stroke engines that are used with portable power equipments like generators or pumps.

Benefits:

  • Has a  very good high oxidation stability and formula that helps in good engine/transmission cleanliness through complete oil drain intervals
  • Anti-wear additive systems helps in protection of engine and gearbox components against wear
  • Develop to control engine deposits and has frictional capabilities for smooth clutch operation
  • Provides high stress protection in extreme working and temperature conditions
  • Helps to minimize oil thinning in gear boxes

Protect the Environment

Please always bring used oil back to an authorized collection point/dealer. Do not dispose into drains, soil or water.

Storage

We suggest to store all packages under cover/protected area. Products must never be stored above 60°C, exposed to hot sun or extreme freezing temperatures.
